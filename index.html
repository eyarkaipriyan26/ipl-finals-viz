Methodology
Late swing score: Σ |ΔWPi| × (i / N−1) — weights each swing by how late it occurs in the match.
Volatility score: standard deviation of the full WP series across both innings.
Both scores normalised 0–100 across all 18 finals, then averaged 50/50.
